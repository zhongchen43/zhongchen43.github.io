Research Interests

  • Statistical signal processing, including statistical modeling of sensor arrays, asymptotic performance analysis of DOA estimators, and the derivation of Cramér-Rao bounds.
  • Convex optimization, including applications to machine learning and sparse recovery in array signal processing.
  • Machine learning, including machine learning methods for graph structured data, kernel-based algorithms, and application of optimal transport in domain adaptation.

About me

I am pursing my Ph.D. at Texas A&M University in College Station and I will graduate in May 2020. My research advisors are Dr. Gregory Huff and Dr. Jean-Francois Chamberland. My research involves statistical signal processing, optimization, machine learning, and phased array design. You can view my publications here.

I am also a Taichi player and do Taichi every day. My master is Taichi Push-hands great master Wentao Lin who is the sixth generation of Yang-style Tai Chi Chuan.
